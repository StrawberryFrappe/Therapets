# Daily Missions

Daily missions turn your healthy habits into game progress. As you interact with the sensor while connected, the background service tracks your "synced minutes".

## Tracking Progress

When your sensor detects activity and the BLE connection is stable, background minutes begin to tally up. This data is regularly synced to the cloud securely.

### What Happens if the Device is Left on a Desk?

If the sensor is left unattended but remains connected, it detects a lack of interaction after the 15-second grace period. At this point:
- The background minute tally is **paused**.
- Cloud logging for activity stops.
- This prevents "ghost" activity from counting towards your daily missions.

## Mission Rewards

Completing your daily minute quotas unlocks rewards in the game, such as food, currency, and accessories for your virtual pet. Be sure to sync your device at least once a day to ensure your progress is recorded!
