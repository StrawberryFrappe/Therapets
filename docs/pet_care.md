# Pet Care & Minigames

Your virtual companion is the heart of Therapets! Completing daily missions allows you to care for and play with your pet.

## Basic Care

Your pet has various states:
- **Hunger**: Use food earned from daily missions to keep your pet fed.
- **Happiness**: Play minigames and interact with your pet to maintain high happiness levels.

## Minigames

Therapets includes several minigames to help you bond with your pet:

### Flappy Pet
A classic side-scrolling obstacle game. Guide your pet through the gaps!
- **Difficulties**: 1 to 4.
- Earning high scores grants bonus rewards.

### Sensor Calibration
Before playing some interactive minigames, you may need to calibrate your sensor:
- Ensure the "Bob" character model is displayed.
- Follow the on-screen instructions in English or Spanish.
- Complete the full range of motion on the bouncing platform to register calibration successfully.

Taking good care of your pet ensures they stay healthy and happy, reflecting your own progress in the real world!
