# BLE Connection Setup

For Therapets to register your activity and progress, you need to connect your smart sensor via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).

## Steps to Connect

1. **Open the app** and ensure your phone's Bluetooth is turned on.
2. Go to the main screen and tap the **Connect Device** icon.
3. The system will start searching for nearby Therapets devices.
4. Select your device from the list. Once connected, the icon will turn green.

![BLE Connection Screen](/assets/images/ble_setup_en.png)

## Troubleshooting

- **If the device disconnects:** Don't worry. The app will freeze your history on the screen (showing "Waiting...") and if you reconnect in less than 30 seconds, everything will resume exactly where you left off.
- **Note:** Cloud information is recorded in real-time, so disconnected time will not add to your progress.
