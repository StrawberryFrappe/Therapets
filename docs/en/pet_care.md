# Pet Care & Feeding

Your virtual pet depends on you. Its hunger and happiness levels are directly related to how you interact with it and the rewards you earn from your missions.

## Earning Food & Feeding

1. **Play & Complete Missions:** Participate in minigames or fulfill your daily missions to earn **Silver Coins**.
2. **The Store:** Go to the store and use your Silver Coins to buy different types of food.
3. **The Fridge:** All the food you buy is stored in the pet's fridge.
4. **Feeding:** To feed your pet, simply open the fridge, select a food item, and drag it to the pet.

> **Tip:** Different foods affect both hunger and happiness levels. Some special foods drastically increase both!
