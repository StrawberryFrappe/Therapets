# Welcome to Therapets

**Therapets** is your smart companion for building healthy habits through gamified interaction with virtual pets.

This guide will help you understand the basics of using the Therapets system, connecting your device, tracking your daily missions, and taking care of your virtual companions.

## Getting Started

If this is your first time using Therapets, we recommend following these steps:

1. **[BLE Setup](connecting_device.html)**: Learn how to connect your smart sensor via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).
2. **[Daily Missions](daily_missions.html)**: Understand how your activity translates into in-game progress.
3. **[Pet Care](pet_care.html)**: Discover how to interact with, feed, and play with your virtual pet.

Use the navigation buttons at the top of the page to browse between sections.
